Enabling Incognito Mode

This extension will only be active in Incognito Mode if it is also enabled in Chrome settings.

Make sure you've manually enabled the extension for incognito access in your browser's settings.

To enable this extension in Incognito Mode, follow these steps:


  1. Open Chrome and click the three dots in the top-right corner.
  2. Go to 'Extensions' and click 'Manage Extensions' in the left sidebar.
  3. Find this extension in the list and click 'Details'.
  4. Scroll down to 'Allow in Incognito' and toggle it on.
